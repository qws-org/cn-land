Fair Play

Our platform is designed to ensure transparency, prevent cheating, and provide a level playing field for recreational players.

✨ 1. Anti-Collusion Measures

We actively monitor for collusion and unfair play. Our security team and automated systems detect suspicious activities, ensuring that no players gain an unfair advantage by working together against others..

✨ 2. Certified Random Number Generator (RNG)

Card distribution is completely random, thanks to our independently tested and certified Random Number Generator (RNG). This guarantees fair and unpredictable outcomes in every game.

✨ 3. Anti AI Policy

We strictly prohibit the use of bots. Our advanced detection systems analyze gameplay patterns to identify and remove any automated accounts, ensuring that you always play against real people. If you notice any unusual player behavior, please report it to security@yuepuke.com, and our security team will respond within 24 hours.

✨ 4. Secure Funds & Transparent Payouts

Your bankroll is safe with us. We use top-tier encryption and security protocols to protect your funds, and all winnings are paid out transparently without hidden conditions.

✨ 5. Player Security & Support

We take player protection seriously. Our platform employs advanced fraud prevention techniques, data encryption, and a dedicated support team ready to assist with any concerns about fair play.
